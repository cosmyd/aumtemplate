Blog

Volunteering experiences are all about discovering and learning - about the world we live in, about the people around us, and about ourselves. Every step is followed by novelty and guided by the motivation to act with kindness and solidarity in order to bring positive change.

As volunteers we are presented with opportunities and challenges that can redefine our comfort zone and expand our perspective. These kind of experiences turn into stories - of connection, of overcoming obstacles, of development and of personal growth - that show that volunteering is an important way of engaging people in solving issues faced by communities.

This space has the purpose of giving voice to these stories and bring together the volunteers in Transilvania.

Visit the blog here: https://www.voluntarintransilvania.com/
